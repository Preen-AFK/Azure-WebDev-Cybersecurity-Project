Hi, I'm Connor! o7

I have always been interested in computers, ever since I struggled to run Counter Strike 1.6 on my dad's Lenovo work laptop. Ever since I left my broadcast media career behind and surrounding myself with the IT space, I have found a deep satisfaction with all the learning and work. I heard highly of UCONN's 6-month cybersecurity program and it was the best risk I took, even greater than my undergraduate degree.

I have hands-on experience with OS's such as Linux, Kali Linux as well as Windows, but the heap of my cybersecurity knowledge lies in Metasploit, Wireshark, Splunk, and Azure. I am familiar with scripting languages such as Bash, Powershell, and VM. By no means am I an expert (yet!).
